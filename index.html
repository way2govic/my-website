Consulting

Jihyun works with founders and executive teams who need to understand how AI is reshaping discovery—and make the right strategic bets before the window closes.

Her focus is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO): how brands show up in AI surfaces like ChatGPT and Google AI Mode. She has designed and implemented the playbook that makes her clients the most visible in their category.

How It Works

Engagements are structured as ongoing strategy conversations.

Clients book a 30–60 minute call each month to:

  • — Pressure-test their AEO strategy and roadmap
  • — Sense-check priorities, resourcing, and sequencing
  • — Get early insight into what's working (and breaking) across AI search
  • — Translate theory into decisions their teams can execute

Speaking

Jihyun Lee speaks to founders, executives, and growth leaders navigating the hardest part of scaling: what to do when the old playbooks stop working.

With nearly a decade of experience building growth engines, Jihyun brings an operator's perspective to topics like AI-driven discovery, audience-led acquisition, experimentation at scale, and sustainable growth systems. Her talks combine real-world examples, clear frameworks, and practical guidance teams can actually apply.

She's spoken on main stages across the U.S. and Europe, and regularly works with leadership teams who want to understand what's changing in growth—and how to respond without chasing hype.
